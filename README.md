## Project 02 — Employee Attrition & Flight Risk Pipeline

End-to-end data pipeline analysis on 1,470 employee records — from raw dirty data to a composite flight risk model. Built to answer: what actually drives people to leave? Pipeline stages: ingest, audit, clean, impute, feature engineering, aggregation, and a weighted 6-factor flight risk scoring model. Key finding: overtime is a stronger attrition driver than compensation, increasing attrition risk by 52%. Built with Python/pandas logic and SQL-equivalent queries.

**Live page:** https://safiatou2coulibaly-sys.github.io/data-portfolio/attrition.html

## Project 03 — Mali Gold: Due Diligence Assessment

Structured open-source intelligence (OSINT) risk assessment of Mali's gold mining sector, covering sovereign risk, sanctions exposure, and reputational risk for entities with ties to Mali's gold supply chain. Draws on U.S. Treasury OFAC designations, ICSID arbitration records, and investigative reporting across 18 primary sources. Key findings include the Barrick–Mali dispute ($430M settlement), Wagner/Africa Corps embedded presence in artisanal mining, and the UAE transshipment corridor as a compliance screening priority.

**Live page:** https://safiatou2coulibaly-sys.github.io/data-portfolio/mali_due_diligence.html
